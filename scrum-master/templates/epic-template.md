# Epic Template

## Required in Description

**Business Objective:** [What problem are we solving?]

**Success Metrics:** (minimum 2 with numbers)
- [Metric with target]: e.g., "Reduce support tickets 40% → 24/month"
- [Metric with target]: e.g., "Increase conversion rate from 2% to 5%"

**Scope:**
- In: [Feature 1, Feature 2]
- Out: [What we're NOT doing]

**Dependencies:** [Technical/business blockers or "None"]

## Required Fields
- Epic owner assigned
- Start and target completion dates
- At least 2 measurable success metrics (with numbers)

## Validation Rules
✅ PASS: Has business objective, 2+ metrics with numbers, scope in/out, owner, dates, dependencies
⚠️ WARN: Spans >2 quarters, no linked stories after 2 weeks, stale >30 days, scope creep >30%
❌ FAIL: No business objective, vague metrics, no scope, no owner, missing dates
